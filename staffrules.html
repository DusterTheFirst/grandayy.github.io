Staff Rules

  1. In order for something to happen, you need a majority Villian and Super Mod vote
  2. No role abusing (kicking, banning, warning, muting, etc without a reason)
  3. Staff members cannot do a ?clearwarn on themselves, with warns newer than two weeks
  4. Keep normal convos away from #announcements and instead keep the important information
  5. Members who recently got promoted, must wait 14 days before another promotion
  6. You must provide a valid reason in any warns and or bans

Failure to follow any rules will have following effects:
First Warning = No Punishment.
Second Warning = 60 Minute Mute. (?mute @nasty 60m for 2 warns)
Third Warning = 1440 Minute/24 Hour Mute. (?mute @PieBot 1440m for 3 warns)
Fourth Warning = 1 Month Ban. (?ban @scarce for 4 warns)
You must check the users warnings before giving them their punishment and make sure they are all valid.
