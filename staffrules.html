Staff Rules

Rule 1: No role abuse (Kicking, Banning, Warning, Muting, etc., without a reason).


Rule 2: Staff members cannot do a ?clearwarn on themselves, with warns newer than two weeks.


Rule 3: Members who just got promoted, must wait 14 days before another promotion.


Rule 4: You must provide valid reason in any warns and or bans.



Failure to follow any rules will have following effects:

First Warning = No Punishment.

Second Warning = 60 Minute Mute.

Third Warning = Kick.

Fourth Warning = One month ban.
